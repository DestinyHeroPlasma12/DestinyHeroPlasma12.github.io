I am A digital Illustrator

I am an Aspiring Illustrator, Animator, Graphic Designer and it seems like right now a web designer. Now what Exactly do these even mean?

Illustrator

Digital Illustrator: A digital illustrator is an artist who uses digital tools, such as a drawing tablet or graphics software, to create illustrations and graphics. Digital illustrators may create artwork for various purposes, including advertising, books, comics, and video games.

Animator

An animator is someone who creates animation using various techniques such as traditional hand-drawn animation, computer-generated imagery (CGI), or stop-motion animation. Animators work in various fields, including film, television, video games, and advertising.

Graphic Designer

A graphic designer is a professional who creates visual content to communicate information and messages to the audience. They may work on a variety of projects, such as designing logos, creating marketing materials, developing user interfaces, and designing websites

Web Designer

A web designer is a professional who creates and designs websites. They use various tools and software to create visually appealing and user-friendly websites. A web designer may work on various aspects of website development, such as creating layouts, selecting colors and fonts, and optimizing website performance for different devices.
